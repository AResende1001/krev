1. Fundamental Structure and Grammar

1.1. Strict Sentence Order (SVO - Subject-Verb-Object)

The standard order is **Subject (S) + Verb (V) + Object (O)**. Verbs are never conjugated.

Mi fren den mets. (I eat the meat.)

1.2. Negation

Use the particle Na after the subject and before the verb.

Vi na sleep. (You do not sleep.)

1.3. Possession (Genitive)

Use the particle De. The structure is: **[Possessed Item] De [Possessor]**.

Den dom de mi. (The house of me / My house.)

1.4. Comparative

Use the structure **[Linking Verb] + Ple [Adjective] Den**.

Den liv zek ple za den den dom. (The book is more big than the house.)

1.5. Yes/No Question (Q)

Add the particle Q at the beginning of the sentence to form a Yes/No question.

Q vi fren den mets? (Do you eat the meat?)

4. Krev Language Philosophy and Biography

Krev is a **hybrid constructed language (Conlang)** created to facilitate communication. It is **in constant and dynamic development**, aiming to maximize **efficiency** and **clarity** in daily use. Its current structure is a simplified synthesis of global influences, mixing simple grammatical rules with a familiar vocabulary derived from various source languages.

Core Foundations and Influences

  • Indo-European Vocabulary: The majority of the core and abstract lexicon is derived from **Germanic** (English, German) and **Romance** (Latin, Spanish, Portuguese) roots, making Krev accessible and familiar to speakers of these families.
  • Minimalist Grammar: The grammatical structure is minimalist, eliminating complex verb conjugation, grammatical gender, and most case inflections. Tenses are marked by simple particles (such as Ke for past and Lo for future), a system simplified similar to the aspect found in some **Slavic** languages.
  • Strict SVO Syntax: The rigid **Subject-Verb-Object (SVO)** word order, along with functional particles (like Q for questions), is inspired by the clarity and lack of ambiguity found in many **Creole Languages**.

Krev was conceived as a practical language, capable of expressing everything from philosophical concepts (Ver - Truth) to everyday needs (Morlan - Breakfast) with a minimum of rules and exceptions, and it continues to evolve based on the needs of its community.
